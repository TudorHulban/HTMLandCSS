Iridiology consultation includes an iris analysis session. Photographing both eyes and analyzing the delicate structures of the iris to locate inflammation in the body and determine how it is manifesting. Nerve fibers in the iris will change color and shape to reflect the health of the body indicating areas of illness, injury, levels of toxic accumulations and where it is located and what stage it has reached - acute, subacute, chronic or degenerative.

Each iris contains over 25,000 nerve endings bordering the pupil connecting directly through the optic nerve which is a bundle of over a million nerves that go to the brain and spinal cord and out to all of the organs and glands of the body. This connection enables us to see the state of body reflected in the eye. Through iris analysis, we can determine organ, gland and tissue weaknesses and strengths. The iris acts as your body’s blueprint. By understanding the information coded in the eye, you can access an empowering road map that will enable you to take steps towards proactive and preventative care, address current manifestations and achieve real, long-lasting, optimal health.
