Testimonials

Adyna and I met over 5 years ago while working on an engineering project together. I was like most of my colleagues working long hours, without breaks and lunches consisted of whatever I could find out of our cafeteria. One day Adyna invited me to go for a walk at lunch to enjoy the refreshing spring weather that was finally here after a particularly harsh Midwest winter. Soon this became a daily routine for us and something I looked forward to every day. As we walked it became very evident that Adyna was very health conscious and followed a strict vegan lifestyle. With Adyna’s guidance I started to change how I looked at foods and slowly began to modify my diet. My diet is not vegan, however following Adyna’s advice I reduced the amount of meat by over 2/3 and more than tripled the amount of fruits and vegetable I eat. The changes were slow at first but with the diet changes and walking I found myself 25 pounds lighter. My energy level increased, even though my daily calorie intake was down significantly. It wasn’t long before I started including weight training to my routine for 3 to 4 days/week. When I’m not at the gym I enjoy early morning rides on the bike. The gym and bike rides are even more enjoyable when Adyna joins in the fun.

Bottom line, if you want to make a life changing effect on your Life, Health and Happiness take Adyna’s advice, I’m glad that I did.
Friends Forever, Bob
