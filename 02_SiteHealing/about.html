Mission ...

Adyna is the creator and founder of Discover Natural Health club. Adyna is a Certified Detoxification Specialist, Herbalist, Iridologist and Nutritionist. She has experience in Nutritional Counseling and Medicinal Herbs. She worked with and researched herbs since the early age of 10. As a child, she always was passionate about fruits, flowers and plants and the calming effect that these had upon her body. She was lucky to have a grandmother and mother both doctors that were passionate about Herbology and Biochemistry, both help her to study the medicinal properties of plants. Her grandmother taught her that there is a plant for every illness and everyone will be able to improve their health by consuming medicinal plants. Adyna is spiritually minded, with a well-rounded knowledge base, and she wants to share her mastery of Detoxification and Iridology to help you cleanse your physical, mental and spiritual bodies, guiding you to true freedom and health to live life to the fullest so you can attend your real purpose.

Her ultimate goal and passion are to have a wellness retreat center in a beautiful setting where she can care and interact with her clients.

As a holistic health practitioner, nutritionist and detoxication specialist, I am here to provide you the knowledge, guidance, and support you need to lead a healthier, more balanced life. I will help you identify the root causes of your health issues, and work with you to improve your conditions, regenerate your tissues and heal your body naturally with food, herbs, psychology and lifestyle practices.

As a holistic health practitioner, I look at multiple areas in your life to address your pain and health concerns. I will support you and guide you through an effective protocol that is customized and tailored to your specific needs as an individual. I advocate a raw vegan and vegan diet with a focus on nutrient and water-rich fruits and vegetable for cleansing.

I Specialize in Detoxification, Chronic Pain, Weight Loss, Hormonal Imbalances, Anxiety and Stress, Adrenal Fatigue, Skin Issues, Digestive Issues, Emotional Eating, Disordered Eating, Blood Sugar Issues, High cholesterol, Low Energy.

Thank you for undertaking this journey with me through the Discover Natural Health, Yours in natural mind, body, and spirit, Adyna.
